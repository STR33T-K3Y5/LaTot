# Carousel Animation Implementation - Task List

## Objective
Implement smooth, direct slide transitions for #about-carousel similar to Backstage.com

## Requirements Met
- ✅ Smooth slide-out/slide-in simultaneous animation
- ✅ No jumps, snaps, or teleporting
- ✅ Fixed side items during transition
- ✅ cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) easing
- ✅ 500ms duration
- ✅ Responsive behavior maintained
